LNW North Route Sectional Appendix Module NW5




NW5007 - MANCHESTER AIRPORT TO HEALD GREEN NORTH JN.
MANCHESTER AIRPORT

Permissive working. All platforms will accommodate one train not exceeding eight vehicles or two trains not exceeding
four vehicles each. If a platform is occupied by more than four vehicles a second train must not be allowed to enter that
platform.
