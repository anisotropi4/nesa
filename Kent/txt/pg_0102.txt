Kent / Sussex / Wessex Routes Sectional Appendix Module KSW1




Civil Engineers ultrasonic test unit and track recording
unit
These units must be driven by a qualified civil engineering department employee. An operations department
Conductor/Driver must be provided when necessary.
The ultrasonic test Unit (comprising DB 977391, DB 999602 and DB 977392) must not exceed 45mph (this speed may be
further reduced to between 20mph and 45mph as required by the Engineer) (code 7Z08) when testing, or 75mph (code
4Z08) when not testing.
The Track Recording Unit (comprising DB 999600 and DB 999601) must not exceed 75mph or 5mph below the maximum
permitted line speed whichever is the lower. It is authorised to run on lines where C1 stock is permitted.
                                              South East (Kent, Sussex, Wessex) Territory GI - Dated: 06/12/08


Class 73 electro-diesel locomotive working on diesel
power
When a train hauled by an electro-diesel locomotive is required to travel over a section of line where electric traction
current is normally available but has been isolated due to incident or engineering works, etc. the following will apply:-
•   Train running with normal load - train times over the section of line concerned to be increased by 50%.
•   Trains running at normal speed – train loading to be reduced by 60%.
