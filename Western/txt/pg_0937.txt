                              Western Route Sectional Appendix Module WR2



GW401 - ASHCHURCH (INCL.) TO WESTERLEIGH JN
ASHCHURCH FOR TEWKESBURY

This station can accommodate four passenger-carrying vehicles. Multiple unit trains formed of more than four vehicles
must use selective door operation (SDO) equipment where fitted. On multiple unit trains not fitted with SDO, guards must
ensure passengers only have access to vehicles that will be adjacent to the platform. Drivers of multiple unit trains that
exceed four coaches must stop with the front four vehicles platformed.
