                      Western Route Sectional Appendix Module WR2



       GW910 - CRAVEN ARMS JN TO LLANDEILO JN
       Pantyffynnon LC (MG)
       The gates are released by Annett's Key with a separate key for each gate lock. The keys and the Annett's key release
       instrument are in a cabinet on the station platform. The cabinet is locked with a BR 1 key.
       The person responsible for operating the gates must contact the Pantyffynnon Signaller to obtain release of the Annett's
       key.
