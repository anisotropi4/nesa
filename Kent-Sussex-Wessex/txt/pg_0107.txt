       Kent / Sussex / Wessex Routes Sectional Appendix Module KSW1




       Class 73 electro-diesel locomotive working on diesel
       power
       When a train hauled by an electro-diesel locomotive is required to travel over a section of line where electric traction
       current is normally available but has been isolated due to incident or engineering works, etc. the following will apply:-
       • Train running with normal load - train times over the section of line concerned to be increased by 50%.
       • Trains running at normal speed – train loading to be reduced by 60%.
