Pre-assembly depot and motive power depot.
The Person in Charge of a movement which requires to pass the ‘Stop’ board towards position light signal L1301 at the exit
from the pre-assembly depot and motive power depot sidings and return behind a ‘Stop’ board must, when the movement is
complete and again on the depot side of the board, advise the Signaller.
