GW105 - UFFINGTON TO FORDGATE VIA BOX
Kingsland Rd Sidings GF

The Person in Charge (PiC) of any movement to/from these sidings must make sure that all hand points are correctly set
for the movement, and that an incoming movement can pass clear of the main line without delay.
The maximum length of any movement must not exceed 45 SLU (288 metres / 945 feet).
The PiC must get permission from the signaller before a movement is made from any siding towards position-light signal
BL6681.
The stabling of vehicles in siding 7 (Arrival/Departure siding furthest from the Down Main line) is prohibited.
The PiC of any movement due to arrive in the sidings must reach a clear understanding with the PiC of any movement
already taking place in the siding.

High Level Siding
The driver must get permission from the signaller to make any movement towards position-light signal BL6704. Out and
back shunting movements towards Barton Hill Depot along the link line are not permitted unless the movement concerned
can stand in rear of, and reverse behind, position-light signal BL6687. The signaller must get permission from the PIC at
Barton Hill Depot before the movement starts.
In/Out Road siding (adjacent to platform 15)
The driver must get permission from the signaller to make any movement towards position-light signal BL6710.
