Handling of "One Train Working" staff. The "One Train Working" staff is retained in a release instrument controlled by the
Truro Signaller, located on the Bay platform at Truro, and from which it must be obtained or returned to by the staff below:-

Trains to or from Bay or Main line platforms -               Driver
Trains to or from Up sidings -                               Shunter or Person in Charge

instrument.
