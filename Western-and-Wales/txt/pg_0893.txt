GW200 - DIDCOT TO HEYFORD
APPLEFORD

Stone sidings
From the northerly direction the sidings are numbered:
         No. 1 Freightliner ) with overhead gantry
         No. 2 Freightliner )
         No. 3 ARC
         No. 4
         Cripple siding (off No. 4)
Ingoing trains or locomotives must come to a stand on the reception siding at the “STOP” board.
Before authorising a movement into Nos. 1-4 sidings, the guard or shunter must ensure that the handpoints are correctly
set and the siding concerned is clear for the movement.
Before departure of a train or light locomotive, the guard or shunter must ensure that at least one Freightliner siding and
one other siding are left clear to accommodate the next incoming train and to provide a locomotive run-round facility.
Should it not be possible to provide a locomotive run-round facility, the guard or shunter must advise their operations
control immediately.
Propelling movements to or from the main line are prohibited during fog and falling snow.
