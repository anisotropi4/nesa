Milton (Lansdown International) Siding
Incoming Movements
The Shunter must advise the Lansdown Supervisor prior to departure from Didcot.
The movement must be brought to a stand at the "Stop" board situated 550 yards before the siding gates and the
permission of the Lansdown Supervisor to proceed to and enter the sidings must be obtained using the mobile telephone.
On arrival the vehicles must be detached and secured on No.1 road and the Shunter must tell the Lansdown Supervisor
that the vehicles are in position and ready for collection.
Before authorising a movement beyond the "Stop" board the Lansdown Supervisor will ensure that:-
(i)      the Lansdown shunting locomotive has been stabled clear of any movements to be made;
(ii)     the sidings gates are open;
(iii)    points are correctly set.
Before authorising a movement beyond the "Stop - Await Instruction" boards the Lansdown Supervisor will ensure that:-
(i)      there will be no movement of road vehicles across the sidings when shunting is taking place, by use of portable
         barriers;
(ii)     the provisions of Rule Book, Module SS2 have been complied with.
