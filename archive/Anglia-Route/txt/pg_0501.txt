EA1200 - CLAPTON JUNCTION TO CHINGFORD
CHINGFORD

Starting of trains
The Driver of a train about to depart from the station towards London must operate the “Train Ready to Start” (TRTS)
button for the platform concerned not more than two minutes before the train is ready to depart.
For movements from the station to the Carriage Sidings, the Driver must operate the TRTS button, unless it is a
movement for which the Driver and Shunter are in radio communication, in which case the TRTS button will be operated
by the Shunter.
The Shunter will operate the TRTS button for movements from the sidings and between station platforms.
                                                                                                        Dated: 05/08/06

EA1210 - BROXBOURNE JCN TO HERTFORD EAST
HERTFORD EAST

Starting of Trains
During out-of-course working, two minutes before a train is ready to depart, the Driver must operate the “Train Ready to
Start” plunger for the platform concerned.
                                                                                                        Dated: 05/08/06
