Scotland Route Sectional Appendix Module SC10



SC147 - BERWICK TO HAYMARKET WEST JN (VIA WAVERLEY)
DUNBAR

Drivers of ECML trains timetabled via the Up Main line or the Down Main line at Dunbar at ED498 signal on the Up line, or
ED487 on the Down line, should accept the route if signalled towards the Up or Down passenger loop respectively. There
is no requirement to stop at the signal and advise the signaller in these circumstances. (The Rule Book, Module S7,
Section 1.2 is modified accordingly).
                                                                                                       Dated: 07/12/13

SC147 - BERWICK TO HAYMARKET WEST JN (VIA WAVERLEY)
DREM

Drivers of ECML trains timetabled via the Up Main line or the Down Main line at Drem at EF544 signal on the Up line, or
EF533 signal on the Down line, should accept the route if signalled towards the Up or Down Passenger loop respectively.
There is no requirement to stop at the signal and advise the signaller in these circumstances. (The Rule Book, Module
S7, Section 1.2 is modified accordingly).
