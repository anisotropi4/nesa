       Scotland Route Sectional Appendix Module SC15



       SC203 – INVERNESS TO WICK
       FORSINARD TO GEORGEMAS
       Working by special authority card - If the radio fails in the leading cab of a train requiring to proceed from
       Forsinard to Georgemas, the rear cab radio must be used. The issue of a 'Long Section' token between
       between Forsinard to Georgemas by the rear cab is authorised.

                                                           Dated: 30/01/16

       SC203 - INVERNESS TO WICK
       Halkirk TEP (Down direction only)

       Drivers are exempt from carrying out the ‘loop clear’ procedure after passing Halkirk.
                                                           Dated: 02/12/06

       SC203 - INVERNESS TO WICK
       GEORGEMAS JN TEP To WICK TEP

       Single line block section - The Down direction single line block section is defined as the line between the stop board at
       Georgemas Jn and the designated line buffer stop at Wick.
                                                           Dated:02/12/06

       SC203 – INVERNESS TO WICK
       WICK TO GEORGEMAS
       Working by special authority card - If the radio fails in the leading cab of a train requiring to proceed from Wick to
       Georgemas, the rear cab radio must be used. The issue of a 'Long Section' token between Wick to Georgemas by the
       rear cab is authorised.

                                                           Dated:30/01/16
