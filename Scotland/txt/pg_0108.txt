       INSTRUCTIONS TO DRIVERS
       Rule Book module S5, section 1.1 (4)


       A train must only pass the section signal at danger for shunting purposes if the signaller has given the driver a
       shunting key for the section concerned.


       Where the shunting key is located remotely from the signal box, the driver must obtain the key when
       instructed to do so by the signaller.


       The shunting key must be returned to the signal box or remote location from which it was obtained.
