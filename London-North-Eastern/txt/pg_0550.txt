London North Eastern Route Sectional Appendix Module LN4




LN3615 - HELPSTON JN TO SYSTON SOUTH JN
LANGHAM To OAKHAM

The Down and Up Goods lines between Langham and Oakham boxes are worked in accordance with the Regulations
for working trains over goods lines not worked on any block system (No Block).
Stop signals will normally be maintained at Danger but when cleared, the Driver must understand that the line ahead may
be occupied, even if the signal is cleared without the train being stopped or nearly stopped at the signal.
