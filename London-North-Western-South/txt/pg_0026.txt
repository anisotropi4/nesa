       Section 11 - Emergency bypass switch (EBS)
       If the EBS has been operated in a train formed of more than one unit when working over certain single lines on North West
       & Central Region, the Driver must stop before leaving the single line at the following locations. The driver must check
       that the train is complete and assure the Signaller accordingly before proceeding.
         •  Shelwick Jn (from Ledbury)
       The above is Tokenless Block or One Train Working (without Train Staff) single lines where the controlling Signaller
       cannot observe tail lamps.
