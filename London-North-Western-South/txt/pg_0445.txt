MD101 - EUSTON TO ARMITAGE JUNCTION (EXCLUSIVE)
Primrose Hill Tunnels To Kensal Green Tunnels

The use of any equipment (such as trolleys, rail mounted plant) that may affect the normal operation of axle counters is
prohibited unless the line is protected in accordance with Rule Book Module TS1 or T3 and a technician is in attendance
to reset the axle counter equipment.
